Persistence Visual Panel

A visual persistence panel consists of the programming of specific flashes in a certain span of microseconds so that in sincrony with the rotation of a motor an image can be seen drawn simulating the use of more LEDs than originally there are. Its creation entailed the 3D modeling and 3D printing of the structure, the programming in microcontrollers for the LEDs and the calculations in microseconds to show the images. All this with the aim of making a personal project out of the ordinary. (SolidWorks) (3D Printing) (Arduino) (MathsSkills)
